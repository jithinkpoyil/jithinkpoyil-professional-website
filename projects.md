  <p>

    study integrated MOVES and EMFAC outputs and compared results against NEI 2020 patterns.
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li>Focus: model differences, fuel scenarios, NOx, hydrocarbons, CO, vapor emissions, brake/tire wear.</li>
    <li>Methods: MOVES, EMFAC, NEI comparison, scenario analysis.</li>
    <li>Use: transportation emissions planning and inventory interpretation.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section id="source-apportionment" class="project-card">

  </ul>
</section>
